Our Locations

We have two locations in the world allowing for non-stop operations. Our first location, based in the San Francisco Bay Area is in the heart of a dynamic biotechnology community. Our highly experienced synthetic chemists work in fully modern laboratories equipped with all the tools necessary for a high-intensity medicinal chemistry campaign including NMR, LC/MS, HPLC, automated chromatography systems, microwave reactors and more.

Our second location mirrors our U.S. site and is located in Taiwan. Our staff is highly experienced in all aspects of modern synthetic organic chemistry and our site is superbly equipped with all the modern infrastructure and tools available to our U.S. staff.

 

Why Taiwan?

Taiwan provides a unique option to the large discovery partners in China and India. With both a highly educated and talented workforce, sophisticated infrastructure and superior operations which include vigilant IP protection and IT security, Taiwan may be the alternative you are looking for to protect your valuable discovery efforts and maximize their potential.
